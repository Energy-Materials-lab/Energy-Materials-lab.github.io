# Research at the Energy Materials Laboratory (EML)

The Energy Materials Laboratory (EML) at the American University in Cairo focuses on the design, synthesis, and characterization of nanostructured materials for energy conversion, storage, and sustainability. Our work bridges fundamental science and practical applications, combining experimental and computational approaches.
